How it Happens

This is the behind-the-scenes process that takes place every time we collect new
medications or supplies.

Collection

It starts with us contacting medical offices. We also reach out to clinics for supplies. Once they agree to help our cause, we proceed. We arrange a time for collection. Our team then gathers the supplies. This collaborative effort ensures we have what we need.

Inventory

We check the expiration date on each medication. This ensures they are within the guidelines of the medical mission. We also verify compliance with the area's donation guidelines. Every medication is inspected thoroughly. This process helps maintain safety and effectiveness. Our careful review ensures all donations meet the required standards.

Distribution

We donate the supplies to different missions. These missions then distribute the items to underdeveloped regions. Another option is for us to deliver those supplies firsthand. We visit villages and areas in need. This allows us to directly see the impact. It also ensures the supplies reach their intended destination.
