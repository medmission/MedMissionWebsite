Kenya - Delivering Hope: Transforming Healthcare Across Communities

In July 2023, MedMission embarked on a transformative mission to Nairobi, Kenya, delivering vital medical supplies to underserved healthcare facilities. Our goal was to provide immediate relief while laying the foundation for long-term healthcare improvements in one of East Africa’s most resource-constrained regions. By addressing urgent needs and collaborating with local communities, we aimed to create lasting change.

USA - Transforming Lives After Hurricanes Helene and Milton

In December 2024, we traveled to North Carolina in partnership with Frontline Outreach to provide vital relief to those affected by the devastating impacts of Hurricane Helene and Hurricane Milton. Our mission was to offer immediate assistance to the communities that were severely impacted, as they navigated the aftermath of these natural disasters.
