Transforming Lives After Hurricanes Helene and Milton

December 2024

In December 2024, we traveled to North Carolina in partnership with Frontline Outreach to provide vital relief to those affected by the devastating impacts of Hurricane Helene and Hurricane Milton. Our mission was to offer immediate assistance to the communities that were severely impacted, as they navigated the aftermath of these natural disasters.

During our mission, we donated over 800 sterile goods and medications to families in need. These supplies were essential in addressing the critical shortages that arose in the wake of the hurricanes. Our team worked tirelessly to distribute resources to ensure that every family received the care they urgently required.

Many families, who had been forced to leave their homes due to the destruction caused by the storms, were grateful for the supplies provided. Though their lives had been uprooted, they were more than willing to accept the support offered, knowing it would help them recover and rebuild. We partnered closely with local outreach organizations to ensure that the distribution process was as efficient and effective as possible, reaching those most in need.

Through collaboration with Frontline Outreach, we were able to help families get back on their feet, providing immediate relief while reinforcing the importance of community and compassion during times of crisis.
